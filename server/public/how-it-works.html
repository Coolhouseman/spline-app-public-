How Spline Works

Spline helps friends in New Zealand split expenses without awkwardness. Create a split, invite friends, track balances, and settle up securely in NZD.

1) Create a split

Add the total amount, describe the expense (rent, groceries, dinner), and choose who’s involved.

2) Choose how to split

Split equally, set custom amounts, or adjust shares so everyone pays what’s fair.

3) Track who owes what

See balances instantly, with clear history so you’re never guessing who paid last time.

4) Settle up securely

Friends pay their share and you can withdraw to your NZ bank account when you’re ready.

  • Best for: flatmates, group trips, shared groceries, dinners, and events.
  • Made for NZ: local currency (NZD) and secure NZ bank connections.
