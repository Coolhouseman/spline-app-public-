Everything You Need to Split Bills

Spline handles the awkward money stuff so you can focus on making memories with friends.

Friend Groups

Add friends by their unique ID and create groups for flatmates, trips, or regular dinner buddies.

Flexible Splitting

Split equally or set custom amounts. Upload receipt photos and add notes for each expense.

Instant Payments

Connect your bank securely via BlinkPay and pay your share with a single tap.

Smart Reminders

Automated daily reminders help friends remember to pay, so you don't have to chase them.

Digital Wallet

Keep funds in your Spline wallet for quick payments or withdraw anytime to your bank.

Clear History

See all past splits, payments, and balances at a glance. Never lose track again.

How Spline Works

Getting started is easy. Split your first bill in minutes.

1

Sign Up

Create your account with your phone number and set up your profile.

2

Add Friends

Find friends using their unique Spline ID and connect instantly.

3

Create a Split

Enter the expense details, choose how to divide it, and invite participants.

4

Get Paid

Friends pay their share to your wallet. Withdraw to your bank anytime.

Bank-Level Security

Your money and data are protected by the same security standards used by banks.

Encrypted Transactions

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.

Secure Authentication

Phone verification and secure password requirements protect your account.

BlinkPay Partnership

Payments are processed through BlinkPay, a licensed NZ payment provider.
