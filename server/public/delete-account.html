Delete Your Account

Request permanent deletion of your Spline account and all associated data.

Before you continue, please note:

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted:

  • All your personal information will be permanently removed
  • Your wallet balance will be forfeited (please withdraw first)
  • All transaction history and split records will be deleted
  • Your friends list and pending splits will be removed
  • Any earned XP, badges, and level progress will be lost

What happens after you submit?

1. We will verify your identity using the email associated with your account.

2. You will receive a confirmation email within 24-48 hours.

3. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

4. Transaction records may be retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations.

Must match the email registered with your Spline account

Request Submitted

We have received your account deletion request. You will receive a confirmation email at the address provided within 24-48 hours with further instructions.

Need help instead?

If you're experiencing issues with your account or have questions, we're here to help. Contact our support team before deleting your account.
