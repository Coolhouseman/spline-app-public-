Split Bills in NZ (Without Chasing Mates)

Spline is a simple bill splitter app for New Zealanders. Track shared expenses, send reminders, and settle up securely in NZD.

When to use a bill splitter

  • Dinners: split evenly or by what each person ordered
  • Group trips: accommodation, fuel, activities, and groceries
  • Shared subscriptions: rotate who pays, or keep it even
  • Events: tickets, gifts, and group purchases

Why Spline is built for NZ

  • Local-first: designed for New Zealand users and NZD
  • Clear balances: always know who owes what
  • Fast settling: friends can pay their share and you can withdraw to your NZ bank
